My story

A short, honest version of how I think and what I like building.

I’ve always been drawn to the “why, how and what if” behind systems. Why work repeats, how do errors occur, and what if we would make the process smoother. That curiosity pulled me into automation, data work, and building tools that remove friction and improve accuracy

In my career, I’ve worked in operations environments where small improvements matter, its alll a part of an eco system where a small change in the bottom pyramid can affect big data reporting or Client information. I like turning these unclear workflows into something measurable, structured, and easy to maintain. A technology that can be trusted and can be used by the next person to achieve the same results.

Right now, I’m building my main project: an online store project where I’m experimenting with an assistant that helps customers choose products.

How I like to work

  • Clarity first: define the problem and the path where are coming from and where we ust go then build the simplest useful version.
