About Us

Here are some of the reasons why you should visit the Country of Zimbabwe during the Summer Holidays!!

Zimbabwe is home to one of the largest and most beautiful naturally occuring falls in the world called the Victoria Falls or Mosi-Oa-Tunya which translates to the Smoke that thunders. This name is backed up by the fact that due to the pressure exerted by the waterfalls the Victoria Falls are always rumbling and are surrounded by an evergreen rainforest which is a sight to behold.

Our lovely nation has in total eleven National Parks, each of which offers vastly contrasting and fulfilling wilderness experiences. We are home to many wild animals some which are endangered and some which you cannot find in Europe, Asia or America.

While having National Parks and Wonders Of the World, Zimbabwe is home to some of the finnest and most skilled dry stonewalling in the world. Great Zimbabwe, Khami Ruins these are all examples of the phenominal works done during the dry stone age and are still standing up to this day. The Great Zimbabwe also during its prime years was the Biggest trade center in all of Southern Africa

To visit this beatiful country you don't even need to do a lot of complicated visa Applications, Visas for all foreigners not from SADC is offered at the Airport through a fast and uncomplicated way costing only up to 50 USD for 30 days of visiting.

When it comes to the residential part yu dont even have to worry, All Tourist Attraction Sites have 5 star accommodation rooms and lodges and waiters and waitresses who will help you with everything you need for your stay. Also when you go for exploration you always have guides who are very skilled and well trained.
